### Q2: Tell us about a time you ran a customer onboarding or technical best-practice session end-to-end.
> **At Amodeling** (our AI SaaS, #8 on Product Hunt) I ran every customer onboarding personally. The pattern looked like: scope the use case in a 20-minute call, build them a bespoke demo for their specific content, walk them through their first successful 3D model generation live, document what broke, feed that into the next customer's onboarding template. By the time I'd done five of these I had a repeatable playbook — the sixth customer onboarded in half the time of the first. The reflection I carry: **the best onboarding is the one that documents itself** — every recurring friction point is a missing default or a missing doc, not a missing CSM. That's the muscle I'd bring to Encord's book.
